# Intro to 3D

### Coordinate Axis
- X-axis : also known as `length`
- Y-axis : also known as `width`
- Z-axis : also known as `Height` or `Depth`

### 4D
The Fourth dimension is known in physics and some theories. Many scientist make the `time` as a fourth dimension.
